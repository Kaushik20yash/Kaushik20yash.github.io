Welcome to the 0_Trace Mine Calculator! Follow the steps below to accurately calculate your coal mine's carbon footprint:

  • Navigate Through Tabs:
    The calculator is divided into different sections such as Drilling & Blasting, Excavation, Loading & Hauling, and others. Click on each tab to enter data specific to that process or activity in your coal mine.

  • Fill in the Required Fields:
    For each process, provide data such as fuel consumption in liters, electricity usage in kWh, and other relevant inputs. Enter numeric values only.

  • Save Your Data:
    After filling out the required fields for each tab, click the "Save" button to store your data.
